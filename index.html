Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Innovation is the spark that turns imagination into reality, driving progress and transforming the way we live, work, and connect.

Sustainability

Sustainability is about meeting today's needs without compromising the ability of future generations to meet theirs

Infrastructure

From roads to digital networks, it’s the invisible force that powers progress and improves the quality of life for all.

Empowerment

Empowerment is about giving individuals the tools, confidence, and opportunities to take control of their lives and reach their full potential.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 envisions a future of sustainable growth, technological innovation, and inclusive development, transforming the nation into a global leader in resilience and progress.With a focus on digital transformation, green energy, and youth empowerment, Bangladesh is paving the way for a brighter, self-reliant tomorrow.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

"I strongly believe that we can create a poverty-free world, if we want to.... In that kind of world, [the] only place you can see poverty is in the museum. When school children will be on a tour of the poverty museum, they will be horrified to see the misery and indignity of human beings. They will blame their forefathers for tolerating this inhuman condition to continue in a massive way."

Dr.Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
